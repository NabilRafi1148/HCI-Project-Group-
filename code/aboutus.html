Our History


Founded in December 2021, FArbucks is a coffee company. Its branches may be found all over the globe. Our team consists of individuals who are always learning and innovating in order to provide the greatest coffee and beverage products available.

We know that great coffee comes from the best coffee beans from around the world. We travel to various corners of the world and work with coffee farmers and roasters around the world to get the best coffee taste.

Our Innovation


We create a printer machine that can print photos / images on a copy so that customers can print their images or photos on their coffee. Our printer machines use natural inks made from coffee, malt, and carrots so that they produce colors from natural extracts and are safe for consumption. Our printer ink has no taste so it will not affect the taste of your coffee.

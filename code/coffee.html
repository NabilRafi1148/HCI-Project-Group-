Colombia Narino Coffee

IDR 30.000

Colombia Narino Coffee is from
Colombia. This premium coffee is
hand-picked by farmers on small
family farms in Colombia's Nario
state, near the Pacific coast.

French Roast Coffee

IDR 25.000

French Roast Coffee is the world’s
darkest roast that has rich flavor of
caramelized sugars, a hint of chocolate,
and a profoundly smokey flavor.

Kona Coffee

IDR 35.000

Kona Coffee is grown at 2,000 feet
above the Kona Coast and recognized
worldwide for their rich aroma,
gentle, delicate taste, and thick body.

Mocha Java Coffee

IDR 40.000

Mocha Java Coffee is the world’s
original coffee blend which has sweet,
fruity Ethiopian Yirgacheffe with the
deep body and rich flavor of
Indonesia's Java Estate coffees.

Cortado Coffee

IDR 25.000

Cortado Coffee comes from Spain and
consisting of espresso mixed with a
roughly equal amount of warm milk
to reduce the acidity. Cortado coffee
has rich taste, smooth and silky.

Viennese Coffee

IDR 35.000

Viennese coffee combines select
coffees from Costa Rica and Brazil
for a cup of satisfying balance
and depth.

Ristretto Coffee

IDR 25.000

Ristretto coffee is a shot of espresso
coffee but extracted with half the
amount of water which create
delicious and concentrated shot.

Latte Coffee

IDR 35.000

Latte coffee is a espresso shot filled
with steamed milk and with a layer
of foamed milk cream.

Cafe au Lait

IDR 27.000

Cafe au Lait is made with French
press coffee with a mix of scalded
milk to bring different flavors in
the coffee. Cafe au Lait has a
distinctive, strong, and bitter flavor.

Cappuccino

IDR 30.000

Cappuccino is made with shot of
espresso, steamed milk, and milk
foam. Cappuccino has a smooth,
slightly sweet taste with a lightness
from the foam.

Irish Coffee

IDR 40.000

Irish coffee is made from strong
brewed coffee with a shot of
whiskey, brown sugar, and
whipped cream topping. Irish coffee
has a sweet, creamy taste with
strong flavors of coffee and whiskey.

Americano

IDR 30.000

Americano is a single or double
shot of espresso where the espresso
is diluted by combining with boiled
water. Americano has nutty and
earthy flavors as the high
temperature destroys floral and
lighter notes.

Macchiato

IDR 28.000

The Macchiato is an espresso based
coffee that contains a dollop of
foamed milk. Macchiato has
creamy and pleasant taste.
